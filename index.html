About Stromboli

The only cabinet with
a true lifetime promise.

At Stromboli, we've been building kitchen cabinets in Bangalore for decades. Our cabinets are crafted from high-grade aluminium alloy — a material that resists moisture, pests, and time far beyond wood or MDF. We don't just build cabinets; we engineer kitchens that become the heart of your home.

"We redesign until you're satisfied — and we build until it lasts a lifetime."

Aluminium Alloy Construction

Unlike wood, our cabinets won't warp, swell, rot, or attract pests. They maintain their structural integrity for decades with minimal maintenance.

Fast Turnaround

From measurement to installation, we deliver on schedule without compromising on quality.

CAD-Designed Precision

Every kitchen is modelled in 3D before fabrication — you see exactly what you'll get.
