How It Works

Our Process, Step by Step

From your first call to the day your new kitchen is ready — here's exactly what to expect when you work with Stromboli.

1 Measurement 2 Quotation 3 Fabrication 4 Installation ? FAQ
Step 01 — Measurement
1
First Visit

We come to you — free of charge.

Everything begins with a visit to your home. One of our trained technicians will measure your kitchen from floor to ceiling, noting every pipe, window, door frame, and appliance position. This ensures the cabinets we design will fit perfectly — not approximately.

Floor-to-ceiling dimensions

We record wall heights, ceiling height, beam positions, and floor levelness — critical for precise cabinet fit.

Pipe and electrical mapping

We identify where water supply, drainage, and electrical points are — so cabinets never block access or violate safety clearances.

On-the-spot notes and photos

Our technician will photograph your kitchen and note your preferences, appliance sizes, and any special requirements.

Step 02 — Quotation
2
CAD Design & Pricing

See your kitchen before it's built.

Using the measurements taken, our designers create a detailed CAD drawing of your proposed kitchen layout. You'll receive a complete 2D plan showing every cabinet, drawer, and shelf — alongside a fully itemised, transparent quotation.

Detailed CAD floor plan

A to-scale drawing showing base cabinets, wall units, appliance spaces, and traffic flow. You can request revisions before anything is built.

Itemised, no-surprises quote

Every cabinet, fitting, hinge, and accessory is listed separately. No hidden charges — what you see is what you pay.

Unlimited design revisions

We redesign until you're completely satisfied — no charge for revision rounds. Only when you approve the plan do we proceed to fabrication.

Step 03 — Fabrication
3
Workshop Build

Precision-built in our Bangalore workshop.

Once you approve the design and confirm your order, our craftsmen get to work. Every cabinet is cut, welded, and assembled in our workshop using high-grade aluminium alloy — a material that outlasts wood, MDF, and plywood by decades.

High-grade aluminium alloy

Unlike wood or MDF, aluminium won't warp, swell, rot, or attract termites — even in humid Indian kitchens.

CNC-precision cutting

Every panel is cut to the exact dimensions from the CAD plan — tolerances are measured in millimetres, not centimetres.

Quality-checked before delivery

Each module is inspected for fit, finish, and mechanism operation before it leaves our workshop.

Step 04 — Installation
4
On-Site Fitting

Your kitchen, ready to use.

Our installation team arrives on the agreed date with all pre-fabricated modules. Cabinets are fixed, levelled, and finished on-site. We clean up after ourselves and walk you through every feature before we leave.

Single-day installation (typically)

Most kitchens are fully installed in one working day. Larger or complex layouts may require two days — agreed in advance.

Level, plumb, and aligned

We use laser levels to ensure every cabinet is perfectly horizontal and vertical — no wonky drawers, ever.

Post-installation walkthrough

We demonstrate every mechanism, confirm you're satisfied, and hand over care instructions before closing the job.
